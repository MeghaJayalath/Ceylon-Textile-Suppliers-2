Company Profile

Ceylon Textile Suppliers

Ceylon Textile Suppliers (CTS)Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of fashion fabrics, down jacket fabrics, functional fabrics etc., headquartered in Melbourne, Australia. One of the largest fabric production and distribution centers in Melbourne. We have been in the textile fabric field for more than 15 years and are top 10 in this field for more than 10 years in Australia, we are devoting ourselves to create a brand paragon for textile industry since its establishment. Our products take the middle and high-end line, we continue to develop new varieties and have guided the trend of fashion and functionality. We are now a comprehensive textile fabric company of collection of weaving, dyeing and finishing, sales. After years of development, our company has formed own quality control system higher than the industry standard and reasonable price competitive mechanism, and has established a deep friendship and win-win business relationship with the vast number of customers at home and abroad.
